Migration Services

Once the documents have been modeled and the templates completed, all existing content needs to be imported into the system. We work with you to retrieve your existing content from other content management systems, databases, or files. We format the content into well-formed, validated XML and import it into Bricolage via its SOAP interface. We ensure that existing links between documents remain intact, so that the newly imported documents can be republished from Bricolage without difficulty.

Contact sales@kineticode.com to get an estimate for migrating your existing content to Bricolage.
