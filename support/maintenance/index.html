Software Maintenance

The Kineticode Software Maintenance plan will help to keep your Bricolage server up-to-date. The plan includes up to five minor Bricolage release upgrades and one major Bricolage release upgrade to be performed by Kineticode over a period of up to 12 months. As part of each upgrade, Kineticode will back up your existing installation of Bricolage, upgrade the server, restart Bricolage, and verify that everything is operating correctly.

The cost for this plan is $4500, or $3500 when purchased in tandem with a Bronze or Silver technical support plan. Software maintenance also comes free with a Gold or Platinum technical support plan. Contact sales@kineticode.com for more information or to purchase a plan.
